About SBI

SBI Mutual Fund is an asset management company sponsored by State Bank of India. It was founded in 1987 with its corporate headquarters located in Mumbai, India. SBI Funds Management Private Limited (SBIFMPL) has been appointed as the Asset Management Company of the SBI Mutual Fund. SBIFMPL is a joint venture between the State Bank of India, an Indian public sector bank, and Amundi, a European asset management company.
